# Test-Day Checklist

Use this for the final day before the IELTS General Training test.

## Documents

- Confirm test date, time, and location.
- Prepare the required ID.
- Check transport time and arrival time.
- Check allowed items.

## Before The Test

- Sleep properly.
- Eat something safe and familiar.
- Avoid learning new strategies.
- Review only short mistake notes and checklists.

## During The Test

Listening:

- Read the next questions before the audio starts.
- Watch spelling, plurals, and word limits.

Reading:

- Do not spend too long on one question.
- Follow word limits exactly.
- Use only information from the text.

Writing:

- Task 1: cover all bullet points and use the right tone.
- Task 2: make the position clear and use clear paragraphs.
- Save time to check grammar and spelling.

Speaking:

- Answer directly, then add one or two details.
- Do not memorize full answers.
- Ask for repetition if needed.
