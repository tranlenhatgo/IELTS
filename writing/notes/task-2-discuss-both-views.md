# Task 2 Discuss Both Views

Skill: Writing
Part: Task 2 essay
Question type: `Discuss both views and give your opinion.`

## Position

You must explain both views, but your opinion still needs to be clear.

Best control: discuss View A, discuss View B, then make one side stronger.

## Structure

| Paragraph | Job |
|---|---|
| Introduction | Paraphrase both views + give your opinion. |
| Body 1 | Explain the view you do not fully support. |
| Body 2 | Explain the stronger view and link it to your opinion. |
| Conclusion | Restate your final judgement. |

## Fixed Frames

Introduction:

`Some people believe that [view A], while others argue that [view B]. I believe [your opinion] because [main reason].`

Body 1:

- `Those who support [view A] may argue that...`
- `This view is understandable because...`
- `However, this argument is limited because...`

Body 2:

- `The opposing view is more convincing because...`
- `This is especially important when...`
- `For this reason, I believe [view B] is the better position.`

Conclusion:

`In conclusion, although [view A] has some merit, I believe [view B] is more convincing because [reason].`

## Band 8 Focus

- Do not write a neutral essay if the question asks for your opinion.
- Put the weaker side first, stronger side second.
- Explain why your side is stronger, not just what both sides say.
- Use objective groups: `parents`, `schools`, `governments`, `children`, `society`.

## Forecast Examples

| Topic | View A | View B |
|---|---|---|
| School transport | government responsibility | parents' responsibility |
| Global contact | positive effects | loss of national identity |
| Recycling education | taught at school | taught at home |

## Quick Review

Both views must appear.
Your opinion must decide which view carries more weight.
