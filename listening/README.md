# Listening

Use this folder for IELTS Listening notes, timed practice, audio-source references, and mistake tracking.

## Folder Use

- `notes/`: question types, listening strategies, spelling rules, and signal-word notes.
- `practice/`: dated practice logs for sections, full tests, and audio drills.
- `mistakes/`: repeated errors such as plural forms, spelling, distractors, numbers, and map labels.
- `resources/`: links to audio sources, practice tests, transcripts, and vocabulary lists.

## Suggested Focus

- Section 1: names, numbers, dates, forms, and spelling.
- Section 2: maps, plans, matching, and paraphrase.
- Section 3: academic discussion, opinions, and distractors.
- Section 4: lecture structure, signposting, and note completion.
